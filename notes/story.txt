OpenMoo Universe:

Schlock Mercenary Themed:
Schlock Mercenary has a perfect set of interesting and unique alien races to substitute for the ones found in the original MoO

The Vhorwed, being giant apes, could fill the bulrathi's Ground Attack spot.
The Amorphs, essentially nanotech integrated computers gone wrong, are hardy enough (and of the right composition) to replace the silicoids.
Th Ob'Enn are Koala bears of a genocidal nature: They would replace the Mrrshan's space combat affinity
